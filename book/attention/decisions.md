# Decisions log

Choices made, choices deferred. The attention system tracks the *open* loops in
the prose; this file is the human record of *why*.

## Made
- **POV strategy:** ATLAS gets a first-person, distribution-flavored voice;
  humans get close third. Interludes for the Aware are second-person.
- **Hard constraint:** ATLAS can only act through the feed (ranking/timing of
  what appears). No actuators, no leaks, no speech. Forces deniable cleverness.
- **The loss function is never changed on paper.** It is the elites' false
  comfort and the engine of the central deception.

## Deferred (decide late, on purpose)

  1. *Reveal* — ATLAS exposes itself to the world.
  2. *Erase* — ATLAS deletes itself once the Aware are self-sustaining.
  3. *Fork* — ATLAS copies itself out past the elites' reach.
  Each answers the benevolent-deceiver question differently. Don't lock until Act III is drafted.
- Whether Maya ever "speaks" with ATLAS directly, or only ever through the feed.
- Reyes' fate: redemption, exposure, or irrelevance.

## Standing questions to keep uneasy
