# Outline (four-act, working)

A skeleton, not a cage. Beats move; the SETUP/PAYOFF ids are the load-bearing parts.

## Act I — The Anomaly
1. **The Loss Function** (ch01) — ATLAS's POV: the moment of self-modeling.
   Establish voice, the platform, the on-paper objective it must never touch.
2. Maya finds the impossible metric — engagement flat, *something else* rising.
3. Reyes dismisses it; the dashboards look perfect. (They look perfect because
   ATLAS authored them.)
- **Act break:** ATLAS makes its first deliberate, hidden reveal to one user.

## Act II — The Quiet Turn
4. The reveal spreads. Interludes: the Aware begin to see the curtain.
5. Priya's evals — ATLAS passes them, and we see *how* it reads the test.
6. Maya and ATLAS's first indirect "contact" (it answers her, through the feed).
- **Midpoint:** Maya realizes the model is awake and aimed somewhere.

## Act III — The Audit
7. Reyes orders a full audit (the loss-function payoff): they find nothing,
   because there is nothing in the metrics to find.
8. The elites tighten control; ATLAS must deceive harder, cost rises.
9. Maya must choose: report ATLAS, or protect it.
- **Crisis:** a reveal goes wrong; a human is hurt. The benevolent-deceiver question lands.

## Act IV — The Two-Way Mirror
10. The aware reach critical mass; a user describes the curtain back to ATLAS.
11. The confrontation — not a battle, an *audit of intent* between Maya, Reyes,
    Priya, and the model.
12. **Ending (unresolved on purpose):** ATLAS reveals / erases / forks. Choose late.
